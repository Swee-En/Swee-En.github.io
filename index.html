Hi, I'm Swee En!

A creative designer, developer & illustrator

After taking the UI/UX course at my university, I realised that there are many useful applications of design and aesthetics outside of illustrations. The whole process of creating an interface was also extremely fun, especially when it came to prototyping and user testing. Being able to produce purposeful designs was greatly satisfying, and being able to hear vastly different opinions for the same product was highly fascinating.
